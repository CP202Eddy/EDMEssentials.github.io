Martin Garrix

Martin Garrix also known as Martijn Gerard Garritsen is known as a DJ, Record Producer, and Musician. His nationality is Dutch and he was born on May 14th, 1996. His music is typically categorised in ‘progressive house’, ‘electro house’, ‘big room house’, and ‘future bass’ genres. Martin Garrix is deeply influenced by Dutch DJ Tiësto. His career started as an Electronic Dance Music star began when he was only 14. His career launched when he joined Spinnin’ Records. In 2016, he created his own label ‘STMPD RCRDS’ and signed with Sony Music. Martin Garrix was ranked at the No. 1 spot on the DJ Mag’s list of top 100 DJs of the world in 2016. Martin Garrix grew his popularity from his hit song "Animals" from 2014 which has been nominated for seven awards and won three times. His recent popular singles that were released are "In the Name of Love" and "Scared to be Lonely" and these two has both achieved platinum status.
