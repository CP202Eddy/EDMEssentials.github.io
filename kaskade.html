Kaskade

Ryan Gary Raddon or better known as “Kaskade,” is one of the most successful American DJs who specializes in progressive-house. Ryan spent most of his early days as a record store owner and produced small tracks on his small label, Mechanized Records. Kaskade continued to familiarize himself with ever changing electronic music scene and created songs such as “It’s You, It’s Me” and “In the Moment,” in 2003. After collaborations with Toronto’s own Deadmau5, they created “Move for me,” which caused people to take notice of his talent. He signed with Ultra and in 2015 released his most popular album titled “Automatic.”
