Seven Lions

Jeff Montalvo or better known as “Seven Lions,” is an American DJ from California that specializes in trance and melodic dubstep. Jeff’s success picked up extremely fast when he won a competition held by Above & Beyond by remixing their song “You Got to Go.” He continued to gain international success when his released his debut EP, “Days to come.” In 2013, he signed with Cadablanca label and a one year later, he released his next EP titled, “Worlds Apart.” In 2017, Seven Lions released his latest album titled “Where I won’t be Found” is currently on his “Horizons” tour in North America.
