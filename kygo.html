Kygo

Kyrre Gørvell-Dahll also known as Kygo who was born in Bergen, Norway introduced his music to the world through the sound termed “tropical house” that tended to be brighter and more relaxed than the commercial dance music contemporaries. He released his first single “Firestone,” in 2014 and did not only reach number one in Norway but reached the top ten in several other countries. In the following year, Kygo released “Stole the Show” which was his second consecutive number one hit song in Norway. As he released his third one, “Nothing Left”, which has also reached the top of the list in Norway, he decided to debut his full length album, “Cloud Nine”. This was released in may 2016 and quickly climbed the charts in Norway as well. Kygo has collaborated with many stars such as Parson James, Will Heard, Ellie Goulding, Selena Gomez, OneRepublic and many more. He has performed in many different countries in the world’s biggest stages and recently came to Toronto in the summer of 2016.
