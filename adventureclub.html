Adventure Club

Adventure club is a duo of Montreal-based producers, Christian Srigley and Leighton James, who blended the sounds of their inspirations offering a mix of slick EDM and floor-rumbling dubstep. These two first introduced their Adventure club project with remixes and videos upload to the web. They toured across North America in 2012 with no official product yet. While in 2013, they landed gigs at the bigger stages in both Ultra Miami and Seoul, South Korea. That same year, they dropped their debut EP, “Calling All Heroes” which included several popular songs such as “Wonder”, “Gold”, and “Crash.” After releasing these successful songs, their fame continued to rise. Adventure club later released their full length album, Red//Blue, which arrived in late 2016 and included appearances by Delaney Jane, DJ DallasK, and the electro-popstar duo Sondar.
