Toronto Edm Essentials

Toronto EDM Essentials is a webpage started together by Eddy Luo and Jacky Bi. Eddy and Jacky are active individuals in the Toronto EDM scene and are very passionate about music which gave them the inspiration to start this website. As active participants in this genre, we understand the excitement of purchasing tickets to different events. Sometimes we may even purchase tickets to DJs who we don't know at all. Through our content, we plan to create an informational website that can help inform the audience of who the DJs are, what type of music they play and a little background information on them as well.Toronto EDM Essentials looks to provide the community of Toronto up to date information about specific DJ's who will be having upcoming events in local clubs/venues. Thank you for taking the time to browse through Toronto EDM Essentials and be sure to visit frequently to be up-to-date with events around Toronto!
