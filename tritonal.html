Tritonal

Tritonal is an EDM duo from Austin, Texas. It consists of the producers/DJs Chad Cisneros and David Reed. The duo formed in 2008 and and immediately began releasing progressive tracks. The duo had remixed songs from famous artists such as Markus Schulz, Cosmic Gate, Zedd, Hardwell, Cash Cash, and many more. In 2013, they spent majority of their time touring around the world in the biggest stages such as Ultra Music Festival, Electric Zoo, and Electric Dance Carnival. They later collaborated with Cash Cash and released a big hit called “Untouchable” and also Chainsmokers with the song “Until You Were Gone.” The duo recently performed at a Halloween event at Toronto’s Rebel Nightclub and as their popularity continues to rise, they will continue to perform at big stages and hopefully come back to Toronto again.
