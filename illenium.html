Illenium

Illenium is the stage name for the the producer and DJ Nicholas Miller who brings unabashed sentimentality and melodicism to festival ready dubstep and trap. He was originally from San Francisco and he began producing electronic music in 2008 and moved to Denver in 2013 where he performed and released music as Illenium. With his rising popularity, Illenium has performed at festivals including Decadence NYE and Global Dance festival and also had many solo tours for his albums. In 2016, he released his album, “Ashes” with popular songs such as “I’ll be Your Reason”, “Its All on U”, “Only One” and many more. In 2017 he returned with his second full-length album, “Awake”, which has peaked at number seven on the Billboard dance charts. With many popular tracks from this album such as “Needed You”, “Sound of Walking Away”, and “Lost”, Illenium continues to grow in popularity.
